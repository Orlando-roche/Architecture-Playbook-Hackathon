# Reading Paths

Guided learning paths through the Architecture Playbook based on your role and goals.

---

## Path 1: New Joiner

**Goal**: Understand how architecture works at CS-CoE

### Week 1: Orientation
1. `packs/ROOT__01_overview_org_roles.md` - Team structure, roles, and responsibilities
2. `GLOSSARY.md` - Key terms used across the organization

### Week 2: Processes
3. `packs/ROOT__02_processes_reviews_decisions.md` - How decisions are made
4. `ADR_INDEX.md` - Browse recent architecture decisions

### Week 3: Standards
5. `packs/GUIDELINES__guardrails_standards.md` - What you must follow
6. `packs/KB__howtos_top.md` - Common tasks and how to do them

---

## Path 2: Engineer Building a System

**Goal**: Design and build systems that meet CS-CoE standards

### Before You Start

2. `packs/ROOT__03_reference_architecture_patterns.md` - Recommended patterns

### During Development
3. `packs/KB__howtos_top.md` - Developer resources and tools
4. `packs/KB__troubleshooting_pitfalls.md` - Common issues to avoid

### Before Review
5. `packs/ROOT__02_processes_reviews_decisions.md` - Review process
6. `packs/ROOT__04_templates_checklists_examples.md` - Templates to use
